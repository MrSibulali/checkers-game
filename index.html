Checkers

  • Source Code

Players

  • Two players are required to play the game

How to move a piece?

  • Select a piece and click on an empty square to move it
  • Pieces only move diagonally
  • a piece cannot jump unless you beating an opponent's piece
  • a piece won't reverse once you move it
  • once a piece reaches the last row it becomes a king and can move and beat in all directions

How to take opponent's piece?

  • You take their piece only when it's blocking your way by jumping it
  • Select a piece and click on an empty square behind the opponent's piece

How to win the game?

  • You win by taking all your opponent's pieces

How to quit the game?

  • You quit the game by refreshing the page or clicking on the quit button.
